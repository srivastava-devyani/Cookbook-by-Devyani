Banoffee Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 large bananas, ripe but firm, sliced
  • 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Chocolate shavings or cocoa powder for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Crust:
    • Mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar.
    • Press into pie dish and bake until golden brown.
    • Cool completely.
  2. Make Caramel Filling:
    • Heat sweetened condensed milk until caramelized.
    • Pour over crust, spread, and let it set.
  3. Layer Bananas and Cream
    • Arrange sliced bananas over the caramel.
    • Whip heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla until stiff
    • Optionally, add chocolate shavings. Chill for at least 4 hours
